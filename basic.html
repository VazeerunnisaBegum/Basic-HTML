VIRAT KOHLI

Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer who plays Test and ODI cricket for the Indian national team.
He's a right-handed batsman and an occasional unorthodox right arm quick bowler.

  • PERSONAL INFORMATION
    • Born: 5 November 1988 (age 36) Delhi, India
    • Nickname: Cheeku King Kohli
    • Batting: Right-handed
    • Spouse: Anushka Sharma

    Kohli's playing style is aggressive, a trait that extends to his leadership on the field. His captaincy is characterized as proactive, taking decisions and leading the team by example
